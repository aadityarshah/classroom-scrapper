Lecture 10 covered the Frobenius method for solving ODEs around regular singular points. The Cauchy-Euler equation served as a foundational example. Finally, Bessel's Equation was analyzed, leading to the series definition of Bessel Functions of the First Kind, Jν(x)J_\nu(x)Jν​(x).
