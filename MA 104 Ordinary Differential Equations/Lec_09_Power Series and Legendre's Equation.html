Quick Summary​

Lecture 9 introduced power series solutions for ODEs around ordinary points, focusing on Legendre's Equation. We derived its series solutions, identified Legendre Polynomials as special cases, and learned Rodrigues' Formula for generating them. A key takeaway is their orthogonality property over [−1,1][-1,1][−1,1], making them a fundamental basis for polynomial expansions.
