Here is a 3-paragraph Markdown overview for 'Module 2: Learning':

This module provides a foundational introduction to Machine Learning (ML), delving into its core definitions from seminal figures like Arthur Samuel and Tom Mitchell. We will explore the fundamental 'Task, Experience, Performance' framework, illustrating its principles with practical examples such as medical image diagnosis to understand how ML systems learn and improve.

The module then showcases the expansive and diverse applications of ML across numerous domains. From accelerating scientific discovery and automating complex processes to enhancing everyday technologies, ML's transformative potential and widespread utility in various sectors will be demonstrated.

Crucially, this foundational overview also addresses the significant challenges and ethical considerations inherent in ML development. Topics include potential failures, algorithmic bias, and adversarial attacks, underscoring the vital importance of responsible AI practices and ethical design in mitigating risks and ensuring fair and robust ML systems.
