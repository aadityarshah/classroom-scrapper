## 🎓 About

Welcome to your central hub for academic lecture notes. These notes are processed by Gemini AI to provide concise summaries and key formulas directly from your lectures.

:::tip How to Use
1. Click **Courses** in the navbar or the button above.
2. Select your course from the list.
3. Access specific lecture notes, downloads, and summaries in the sidebar.
:::

:::info Formatting
All math is rendered using $\LaTeX$. Notes are strictly limited to lecture content without external examples.
:::
